Learning To Fly R/C Helicopters

Getting a new r/c helicopter is always exciting and so is being able to put it together. Now if building things is not your forte there are many kits out there that can be purchased pre-built and tuned. Regardless of which option you choose it's important that you spend time learning the basics of flying a helicopter in order to minimize crashes and large repair bills.

I hope you enjoyed viewing this website and were able to learn some new things about r/c helicopters. If you have any questions for me please feel free to contact me at Murph146@msu.edu
