Favorite Destinations

Traveling to different places both in and out of the U.S. has always been a passion of mine and I was fortunate to be able to do this from a young age. One of the reasons this was possible was due to company trips my dad was invited to on a regular basis. One of the first trips I ever remember taking was to Hawaii when I was in fourth grade. Other than the 12 hour plane ride at a young age the entire experience was a blast. Since then I have been numerous places with my family and with friends.
