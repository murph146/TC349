Cool R/C Helicopter Pictures & Videos

This is by far my favorite page of the entire site and hopefully yours too. As you'll see flying r/c helicopters are quite a big deal and there is a huge following. Many of the top hobbyist also receive sponorship from the big manufacturers of r/c helicopters and parts.
