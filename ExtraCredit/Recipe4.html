Murphy Family Recipe - A little taste of Italy


Ingredients You'll Need:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 lb. sweet Italian sausage (casings removed)
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed dried red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1 1/2 garlic cloves
  • 1 can of Italian plum tomatoes
  • 3/4 cup whipping cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 6 ounces pasta
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons minced parsley
  • Grated parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a heavy skillet over medium heat
  2. Add sausage and crushes red pepper
  3. Cook until sausage is no longer pink (stir frequently for about 7 minutes)
  4. Add onion and garlic to skillet and cook until onion is tender and sausage is light brown
  5. Add tomatoes, whipped cream, and salt
  6. Simmer until mixture thickens slightly (about 4 minutes)
  7. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water
  8. Drain pasta
  9. Add pasta to sauce and cook until pasta is heated through and sauce thickens (stir occasionally)
  10. Divide pasta between plates
  11. Sprinkle with parsley
  12. Serve! Enjoy!

Conversions

3 teaspoons 1 tablespoon
2 cups 1 pint
