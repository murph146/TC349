Robert Murphy Biography

Rob Murphy is 29 year old IT Assistant from Novi, MI. He studied Media and Communications Technology at Michigan State University in East Lansing, Michigan. His reason for pursing a Bachelor's of Science degree is due to his love of new and cutting edge technology. Some of his other passions are computers, sport bikes, car audio installations, woodworking, and flying single-engine airplanes. In previous years he has worked for Best Buy as a car audio technician and was an IT Manager for UBS Financial Services. His most recent place of employment has been at Michigan State University working as an IT Assistant until he graduates this December.

For the past two years Rob has been a member of the Michigan State University Deans List. During his last year of high school, Rob had the priviledge of winning first place at a State of Michigan woodworking competition. In his free time he enjoyings spending time with his family, working out, playing golf, riding sport bikes, hanging out with friends, and working with new technology.
